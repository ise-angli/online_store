About Us
Beijing Time Q&H Technology Co., Ltd.

Beijing Time Q&H Technology Co., Ltd. adheres to the principle of quality first and service-oriented, actively explores domestic and international markets, focuses on the development of welding power sources, automated welding, and surfacing technology, and continuously introduces new high-tech products to the market, such as online/offline open arc surfacing equipment, digital welding power sources, wear plate surfacing equipment, pipeline inner wall surfacing equipment, various surfacing special machines, and flux-cored wires.

Time Q&H Technology consistently proves through successful cases that providing customers with superior quality, top-tier service, and durable, low-maintenance products will be your choice.

Time Q&H Technology always insists on being user-demand oriented, committed to developing high-performance welding power sources and surfacing equipment products, keeping an eye on industry frontiers, constantly challenging ourselves, and keeping pace with the times.

Time Q&H Technology's sales cover major cities and regions in China, and we have provided many qualified welding equipment to dozens of enterprises including power plants, cement plants, and steel mills.

While expanding the domestic market, Time Q&H Technology is also actively exploring international markets. Currently, our online and offline surfacing equipment has been exported in bulk to foreign markets such as the United States, Canada, Malaysia, Bangladesh, Egypt, Russia, and the European Union!
